Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton for his exceptional driving and record-breaking achievements, while his activism and outspoken nature evoke mixed reactions. His influence in modern F1 is undeniable, whether admired or debated

Max Verstappen

Max Verstappen is praised for his aggressive driving and talent, earning him a strong fan base and championships. However, his style can be polarizing among fans.

Valteri Bottas

Consistent and dependable, continuing to provide solid performances and valuable experience.

Sergio Perez

Reliable teammate to Verstappen, offering strong performances and strategic support.

Carlos Sainz

Reliable and adaptable, working to strengthen his position and contribute to Ferrari’s success.

Nico Hulkenberg

racing for Haas, is valued for his experience and consistency. Despite being a seasoned driver, he continues to demonstrate solid performances and contribute significantly to the team.

Yuki Tsunoda

Aggressive and fast, working to improve consistency and deliver strong results.

Fernando Alonso

Experienced and highly skilled, making a notable return with high expectations for Aston Martin.

Lance Stroll

Developing driver with a strong work ethic, aiming to enhance his performance alongside Alonso.

Franco Colapinto

rising star in motorsport, primarily competing in junior categories and with significant potential. His progress is being closely watched, though he is not yet in a full-time F1 seat.

Charles Leclerc

Charles Leclerc is admired for his skill and speed on the track, with many fans appreciating his talent and potential. However, some feel he still needs to secure more consistent results to fully realize his potential.

Lando Norris

Lando Norris is celebrated for his driving skills and charismatic personality, with fans eager for more consistent podium finishes.

Oscar Piastri

Oscar Piastri is a highly promising rookie driver for McLaren in the 2024 season. Known for his impressive performances in junior categories, Piastri has quickly adapted to F1 and shown significant potential.

George Russell

George Russell continues to impress with his strong performances and adaptability for Mercedes. As a rising star, he's solidifying his position as a key driver and future contender.

Alex Albon

Alex Albon, driving for Williams, is known for his strong racecraft and impressive performances. He's steadily proving himself as a talented and reliable driver.

Daniel Riccardo

Charismatic and skilled, looking to regain top form and make a significant impact with AlphaTauri.

Kevin Magnussen

also with Haas, is known for his aggressive driving and strong race performances. His experience and tenacity make him a key asset for the team.

Zhou Guanyu

Talented and promising, working to establish himself further and contribute to Alfa Romeo’s progress.

Pierre Gasly

Talented and determined, seeking to establish himself as a key player with strong performances.

Estabon Ocon

Known for resilience and solid racecraft, continuing to deliver reliable performances for Alpine.
